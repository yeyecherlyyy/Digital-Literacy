# Task 5 – Cybercrime Awareness

## Overview
This task focused on understanding cybercrime and creating awareness about online safety. It included a case study and a prevention checklist to help students recognize and avoid common online threats.

## Case Study
A phishing attack scenario was used to explain how cybercrime happens.  
It showed how attackers send professional-looking emails, create urgency, and trick users into sharing personal information.

## Prevention Checklist
A “Stay Safe Online” checklist was created with practical tips for students.  
It includes general safety practices and specific precautions for UPI and online payments.

## Key Takeaways
- Cybercrime often happens due to quick decisions and lack of verification  
- Awareness alone is not enough; careful behavior is also important  
- Small habits like checking links and verifying sources can prevent major risks  
- Knowing where to report cybercrime is also important (cybercrime.gov.in, 1930)
