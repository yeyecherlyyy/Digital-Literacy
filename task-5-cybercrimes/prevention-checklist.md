# Stay Safe Online – Prevention Checklist

## General Safety Tips
- Always verify the sender before clicking on links or downloading attachments.
- Do not share personal information like passwords, OTPs, or bank details with anyone.
- Check website URLs carefully (look for "https" and correct spelling).
- Avoid clicking on links from unknown or suspicious emails/messages.
- Think before acting on urgent messages or offers that seem too good to be true.
- Use strong and unique passwords for different accounts.
- Enable two-factor authentication (2FA) wherever possible.
- Keep your apps and devices updated to avoid security vulnerabilities.

## UPI / Financial Safety Tips
- Never share your UPI PIN with anyone, even if they claim to be from a bank.
- Do not approve "collect requests" unless you are sure about the sender.
- Always verify the receiver’s details before making any payment.
- Avoid scanning unknown QR codes, as they may lead to fraud.

## Reporting Cybercrime
If you become a victim of cybercrime, report it immediately:

- 🌐 National Cyber Crime Portal: https://cybercrime.gov.in  
- 📞 Helpline Number: 1930  

Early reporting can help prevent further loss and increase chances of recovery.
