# Social Media Do’s and Don’ts 

| ✅ Do’s                                                           | ❌ Don’ts                                                     |
| ---------------------------------------------------------------- | ------------------------------------------------------------ |
| Think before posting and consider long term impact               | Do not share personal or sensitive information publicly      |
| Maintain a professional image on academic/professional platforms | Avoid posting inappropriate or offensive content             |
| Communicate respectfully and politely with others                | Do not use disrespectful or casual language in formal spaces |
| Verify information before sharing or reposting                   | Do not spread fake or unverified information                 |
| Use privacy settings to control your digital presence            | Avoid posting impulsively, especially in anger               |

## Note:

Responsible social media use helps build a positive digital identity and prevents future risks.
